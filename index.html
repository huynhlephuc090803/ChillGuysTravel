Featured Tour

Da Nang - Hoi An 6D5N

6 days 5 nights tour, starting from Tan Son Nhat Airport to Da Nang Airport. The journey begins at Ba Na Hills, followed by My Khe Beach, Marble Mountains, then exploring Hoi An and Cham Island.

Price: $2500

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival at Da Nang Airport, transfer to hotel, evening at Ba Na Hills.
  • Day 2: Visit Golden Bridge & Fantasy Park at Ba Na Hills.
  • Day 3: Relax at My Khe Beach, seafood dinner by the sea.
  • Day 4: Explore Marble Mountains, transfer to Hoi An Ancient Town.
  • Day 5: Boat trip to Cham Island, snorkeling & local culture.
  • Day 6: Free time in Hoi An, departure to Da Nang Airport.

Pricing

Standard Price: $2500 per person

Discount: 30% off for groups of 10 people
